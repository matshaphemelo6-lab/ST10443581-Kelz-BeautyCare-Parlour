Organisation overview
Introduction 
Name of organisation: KelzCare Beauty Parlour 

History about the organisation
•	This is the history about the KelzCare Beauty parlour which started on the year 2019 when the owner discovered that her passion was in the beauty sector. She then decided to start her own nail salon. She then saw that the nail salon did not generate another enough income where she decided to make her own skincare products and healthcare products for women. 

Mission and Vision
•	The mission of the organisation is to enhance natural beauty and boost confidence by providing a professional and affordable high-quality of services and products to the clients in a welcoming and relaxed environment 
•	The vision of the organisation is to become the most trusted beauty parlour in the community, known for innovation, upliftment of young talent, excellent customer service and making the customer to feel beautiful inside and outside. 
Target audience
•	The target audience of this beauty parlour is:
1.	Young adults
-which is 18-30 years old who are students and young professionals, who are interested in trendy nails, skincare and makeup and are heavy users of the internet and online booking. 
2.	Brides and special events client: women who are preparing for weddings, birthdays, graduations and photoshoots and are looking or full pampering packages
3.	Older Adults (40+ years old): Loyal clients seeking consistent beauty and wellness treatments. Prefer a professional website that reflects trust and credibility.
The proposed website will be able to:
•	To provide an online platform besides social media such as Facebook or WhatsApp
•	To showcase the beauty parlour services, promotions and prices
•	To allow clients to book appointments online
•	To attract potential customer through gallery and comments

Benefits of the website 
The website will include the following features

•	Homepage: the homepage will have a welcoming design and service highlights
•	About Us: the about us will have a story, values, and the team introduction
•	Services page: the lists of services rendered will be mentioned such as nails, skincare with pricing
•	Gallery: which will include photos of different styles, transformation of clients, and treatments. 
•	Booking system: there will be an online booking form with the clients preferred date and time
•	Reviews: the customers comments and feedback
•	Contact us page: where the customers can access our contact details and they can also leave their contacts details and a message
•	Enquiries page: where the customer will be able to enter their enquiries regarding the business

Design Approach
•	Modern and Elegant Look – to match the beauty industry.
•	Mobile-Friendly – responsive design for smartphones and tablets.
•	Fast & Secure – optimized loading speed and SSL certificate.
•	Easy to Update – content management system
